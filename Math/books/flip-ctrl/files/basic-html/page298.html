              Conveyor Drive System Specifications:

              System Requirements:

                  A motor must be selected to mate with existing drive components. The conveyor and
              load must be accelerated from rest to 200 ft/min in 0.5 sec, driven at constant speed for 14
              sec  and  decelerated  to  rest  in  0.5  sec.  For  the  worst-case  analysis,  the  cycle  is  repeated
              continuously.  The  drive  train  description  and  motion  profile  are  shown  in  Figs  1  and  2,
              respectively.
